About the App

KetsuekiGas is a comprehensive educational tool designed to help medical students and healthcare professionals practice and understand arterial and venous blood gas analysis. It systematically evaluates pH, PaCO2, HCO3, and Anion Gap based on established medical algorithms to generate educational differential lists.
